Open Source & Third-Party Licenses

The ajayji app is built using powerful open-source software and third-party libraries. We extend our gratitude to the open-source community and the developers who make these tools possible.

How to view licenses in the app

To view the comprehensive list of licenses, copyright notices, and terms for all software packages used in ajayji, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the ajayji app.
  2. Navigate to Settings > About.
  3. Tap on Open Source Licenses.
